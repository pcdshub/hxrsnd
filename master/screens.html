EDM Screens

System

Within the HXRSnD package there is a system level EDM screen that shows all the aerotech and attocube motors, as well as the pneumatics of the system and each of the delay towers.

To launch the screen from the HXRSnD directory:

$ ./HXRSnD/screens/snd_main

To launch from the released area:

$ /reg/neh/operator/xcsopr/bin/snd/snd_main
